About

ABOUT AMERICAN INSTITUTE OF DIAMOND CUTTING, INC.

  • Over 30 Years Of Quality Education
  • America's State of the Art Diamond Training Facility
  • The Institute is the Only Diamond Cutting school in the United States that is licensed to teach the prestige profession of Diamond Cutting and Rough Diamond Grading in all its phases.
  • "Licensed by the "Florida Department of Education" License #436
  • AIDC has the honor of accepting any U.S. military personnel for training under Veterans Benefit.
  • "Approved by the State Approving Agency for Veterans Training"

We provide services in cutting and polishing rough diamonds, modernize older cuts, and recutting chipped and broken diamonds.

We provide consultation services for field operations in diamond mining, evaluation for large parcels of rough. Setting up and establishing large or small diamond cutting factories.

We provide laboratory testing and identification for rough and polished diamonds.
